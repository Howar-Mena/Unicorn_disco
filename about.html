About Us

Located in the heart of Houston, Unicorn Disco Bar is a vibrant, whimsical hotspot that combines a lively atmosphere with funky, eclectic decor. The bar is known for its colorful interior, featuring neon lights, plush velvet seating, and playful touches like rainbow accents and a giant disco ball. This magical venue offers a unique escape, drawing in crowds looking for a fun, high-energy spot to dance, socialize, and enjoy creative cocktails. With its surreal vibe and retro-inspired design, the Unicorn Disco Bar promises an unforgettable nightlife experience for anyone seeking something a little different.

The music at Unicorn Disco Bar is as electrifying as its decor, with talented DJs spinning a mix of retro, disco, and pop hits that keep the energy high throughout the night. Whether you're dancing on the spacious floor or relaxing with a signature drink, the bar's vibe is always upbeat and celebratory. The drink menu is filled with creative cocktails, craft beers, and playful garnishes, ensuring there's something for everyone. Whether you're out for a casual night out or celebrating a special occasion, Unicorn Disco Bar offers the perfect blend of fun, individuality, and nightlife in Houston.
